Durga Puja

Durga Puja is one of the most popular Hindu festivals in India, especially in West Bengal, Odisha, and Assam. It celebrates Goddess Durga's victory over the demon Mahishasura. The festival is known for artistic pandals, cultural performances, and grand celebrations.
